"The mushroom-choked second wing of Coilfang Reservoir — flooded fungal caverns where Lost Ones tribesmen scrape a living among bog giants and the Black Stalker's brood."

🎯 UB Loot Summary

Equipment Focus: Mid-Coilfang wing — Sporeggar reputation runs, druid-themed leather, and the Black Stalker's levitate-mechanic epics on heroic.

  • Best for: Level 63-66
  • Run time: 35-50 minutes
  • Mob levels: 62-65
  • Famous for: Sporeggar reputation grind (turn in Sanguine Hibiscus from trash), Hungarfen druid leather, Black Stalker heroic epics, Musel'ek + Claw pet fight
  • Includes: Hungarfen's mushroom AoE ramp, Ghaz'an hydra in the central pool, two-target Swamplord Musel'ek + Claw bear-pet encounter
