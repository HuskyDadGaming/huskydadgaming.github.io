🎯 SH Loot Summary

Equipment Focus: Top-tier Hellfire heroic — pre-raid BiS plate/leather, Kargath's blade-fist epics, and Honor Hold/Thrallmar exalted rep tokens.

  • Best for: Level 67-70
  • Run time: 45-70 minutes
  • Mob levels: 69-72
  • Famous for: Kargath's heroic-only epic drops, Grand Warlock Nethekurse caster loot, heroic-only Blood Guard Porung, Thrallmar/Honor Hold rep grind
  • Includes: Heroic-only Blood Guard Porung mini-boss, Warbringer O'mrogg two-headed ogre, gladiator pit trash gauntlets, high-density Shattered Hand pulls
