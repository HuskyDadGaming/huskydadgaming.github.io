🎯 SV Loot Summary

Equipment Focus: Top-tier Coilfang heroic — Warlord Kalithresh epics, Cenarion Expedition exalted rewards, and Serpentshrine Cavern attunement step (heroic Lord of the Reservoir).

  • Best for: Level 67-70
  • Run time: 50-70 minutes
  • Mob levels: 70-72
  • Famous for: Warlord Kalithresh epics (Earthsoul Trousers, Ravenous Amulet), Cenarion Expedition rep cap, SSC attunement step on heroic, distiller-destroy mechanic
  • Includes: Hydromancer Thespia water elementals, Mekgineer Steamrigger repair-add waves at 75/50/25, distiller-interrupt mechanic on Kalithresh
