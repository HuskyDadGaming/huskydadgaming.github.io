👁️ The Arcatraz

"Tempest Keep's high-security void-prison — eredar inmates and worse held in stasis cells; Warden Mellichar's broken containment unleashes Harbinger Skyriss for the finale."

🎯 Arc Loot Summary

Equipment Focus: Top-tier Tempest Keep heroic — pre-raid BiS caster/healer drops, Karazhan attunement step (Second Fragment of the Master's Key), and the Arcatraz Key questline required to enter.

  • Best for: Level 68-70
  • Run time: 50-75 minutes
  • Mob levels: 70-72
  • Famous for: Karazhan attunement step on heroic, Harbinger Skyriss caster epics, Arcatraz Key questline (Shattered Sun / Consortium chain), Dalliah + Soccothrates paired-eredar fight
  • Includes: Warden Mellichar five-wave stasis-cell prison-break event (Millhouse Manastorm cameo), Harbinger Skyriss illusion-split at 66/33%, Zereketh void-zone opener
