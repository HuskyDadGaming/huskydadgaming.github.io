⚔️ Scarlet Monastery

"The fanatical Scarlet Crusade's stronghold — four wings: Graveyard, Library, Armory, and Cathedral."

🎯 Scarlet Monastery Loot Summary

Equipment Focus: The Scarlet Crusade's stronghold — four wings with distinct loot tables. Premier dungeon for leveling 30s and 40s.

  • Best for: Levels 28-40 (varies by wing)
  • Wings: Graveyard (~28-30), Library (~30-34), Armory (~33-37), Cathedral (~38-42)
  • Famous for: Whitemane's Chapeau, Herod's Shoulder, Mograine's Might, Hand of Righteousness
  • Note: Mograine + Whitemane two-stage fight in the Cathedral is the highlight
🛡 Gear sets
Chain of the Scarlet Crusade
Scarlet Belt, Scarlet Boots, Scarlet Chestpiece, Scarlet Gauntlets, Scarlet Leggings, Scarlet Wristguards

  • 3 pieces: Increases defense rating by 2.
  • 4 pieces: +5 Shadow Resistance.
  • 5 pieces: Increases attack power by 15 when fighting Undead.
  • 6 pieces: Increases your hit rating by 10.
