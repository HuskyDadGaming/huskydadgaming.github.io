Scarlet Monastery

A cathedral complex in the Tirisfal Glades held by the fanatical Scarlet Crusade. Four wings on a single instance map: Graveyard (low), Library, Armory, and Cathedral (final). Pick one wing per run — the entrance lobby lets you pick which doors to open. Iconic for the Mograine + Whitemane paired final fight in the Cathedral.

Outdoor cemetery wing — open-air, lots of patrols. Three rare bosses (rank 2) spawn here on rotation: Azshir, Fallen Champion, Ironspine. Bloodmage Thalnos is the wing finisher in the back crypt.

Interrogator Vishas

Sadistic Crusade torturer. Hits hard with shadow fear and a self-heal — kick if you have the option. Not too tough, just keep healers untouchable. Drops the Bloody Brass Knuckles, an early melee twink staple.

📚 Library wing lvl 34–35 · 2 bosses

Scarlet Crusade scriptorium and reading rooms. Shorter than the others — just two bosses. Famous for the Library quest chain (no longer mandatory on most servers but still gives nice XP).

First boss — patrols the entrance hall with two attack hounds. Mind-soothe or sap a dog if you can, otherwise burst Loksey while tank holds the dogs. Drops Dog Training Gloves (60% chance) — best-in-slot mail hands for the bracket.

⚔️ Armory wing lvl 37 · 1 boss

Single-boss wing centered on Herod, the Crusade champion. He summons huge waves of trash on a Whirlwind enrage — pull him back into the training hall and AoE the adds. Drops Ravager and the iconic Raging Berserker's Helm.

Herod

Champion of the Crusade. Whirlwinds at low HP and yells "MORE! MORE BLOOD FOR THE LEGION'S BLOODBATH!" while summoning the entire training hall to attack. Pull him back, AoE the trash, finish him. Drops Ravager (legendary 2H axe, every tank's first proper weapon) and Raging Berserker's Helm (BiS plate head for the bracket).

✝️ Cathedral wing lvl 40 · 2 bosses

The big finale wing — lots of trash leading up to a dramatic boss room. Fairbanks is an OPTIONAL fight tucked off to the side (skip-able). The main event is Mograine + Whitemane: kill Mograine, Whitemane resurrects him, kill her, then finish Mograine again. Ignite the boss-rage on her res — burn through it.

High Inquisitor Fairbanks

OPTIONAL boss tucked in a side room before the main Cathedral. Long RP intro — he was an old friend of Mograine. Skip-able if you're speed-running. Drops a trio of leather/mail bracers and shawl with caster stats. Worth one pull if you've got a healer who needs the Inquisitor's Shawl.
