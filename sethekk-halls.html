🪶 Sethekk Halls

"The Sethekk arakkoa wing of Auchindoun — Talon King Ikiss's cult of corrupted bird-mages worshipping the void god Anzu in the shadow of the dead."

🎯 SH Loot Summary

Equipment Focus: Mid Auchindoun wing — famous for the heroic-only Anzu (Raven Lord) mount and Lower City reputation.

  • Best for: Level 65-68
  • Run time: 30-45 minutes
  • Mob levels: 67-69
  • Famous for: Reins of the Raven Lord (Anzu heroic-only — druid-summoned), Talon King Ikiss arcane caster drops, Lower City rep
  • Includes: Druid-summoned Anzu side boss (heroic only, requires Druid 'Flight Form' quest line), Darkweaver Syth four-element add phases, Ikiss arcane-explosion LoS dance
