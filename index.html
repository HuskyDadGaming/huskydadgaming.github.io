Two level numbers: Mob levels are the actual creature levels in the dungeon, pulled from our server's database. Recommended is the player level bracket where you'll get full XP and appropriate gear drops — usually a few levels wider than the mob range.

About this guide: Loot data is pulled directly from our KCraft database (creature_loot_template + item_template). Drop chances are real values from the server install, not generic Wowhead estimates. Each dungeon page filters out trade goods, vendor trash, quest items, and bags — leaving only equippable gear.

Filters: Each dungeon page includes role filtering (Tank/Healer/Melee/Ranged) and class filtering (all 10 WotLK classes). Combine them to find exactly what you're looking for — e.g. "Healer Druid gear" shows Resto-friendly leather and staff drops.
