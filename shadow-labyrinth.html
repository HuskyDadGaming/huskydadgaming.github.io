🌑 Shadow Labyrinth

"The deepest pit of Auchindoun — the Shadow Council's voidcaller temple beneath the Sethekk halls, where Murmur the sound-elemental was bound."

🎯 SL Loot Summary

Equipment Focus: Top-tier Auchindoun heroic — pre-raid BiS shadow-themed gear, Murmur sonic-boom epics, and the Karazhan attunement step (heroic First Fragment of the Master's Key).

  • Best for: Level 67-70
  • Run time: 45-65 minutes
  • Mob levels: 69-72
  • Famous for: Murmur sonic-boom epics (Bands of Indrajit, Talon of the Tempest), Karazhan attunement step on heroic, Grandmaster Vorpil voidtraveller-summon fight, Blackheart mind-control roulette
  • Includes: Ambassador Hellmaw (banishable Eredar), Blackheart the Inciter party mind-control, Grandmaster Vorpil Rain of Fire teleport circle, Murmur LoS/absorb sonic boom check
