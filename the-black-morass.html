🌀 The Black Morass

"Caverns of Time wing — defend Medivh while he opens the Dark Portal as the Infinite Dragonflight floods the rift with time-rippers across eighteen escalating waves."

🎯 BM Loot Summary

Equipment Focus: 18-wave portal defense — Keepers of Time exalted rewards, Aeonus dragonkin epics, and the Caverns of Time: Hyjal attunement step (heroic-only Medallion of Karabor).

  • Best for: Level 68-70
  • Run time: 35-55 minutes
  • Mob levels: 70-73
  • Famous for: Hyjal-tier attunement precursor on retail (now skippable in 3.3.5a), Aeonus dragonkin epics, Keepers of Time exalted ring, Chrono Lord Deja arcane gear
  • Includes: 18-wave timed portal defense, three named Infinite bosses (Deja wave 6, Temporus wave 12, Aeonus wave 18), protect Medivh's channel or wipe
